Research Interests

My long-term goal is to apply formal methods to solve real-world problems. Currently, my research topics focus on:

  • Design and improve Hardware verification techniques to meet large scale of industrial demands
  • SAT/SMT techniques and their Applications
  • Satisfiability Checking of Linear Temporal Logic and its Applications
  • Synthesis from Formal Specification
  • Automata Theory
